Naval Architect

Viraat
MARINE

We are seeking a dynamic Naval Architect with 3-5 years of professional experience in a marine design consultancy environment. The role involves delivering innovative solutions for vessel design, retrofitting, and performance analysis, while ensuring adherence to maritime regulations and client requirements.

  • Lead and support projects related to vessel design, stability, and performance optimization.
  • Perform detailed engineering analyses, including hydrodynamics and structural assessments.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to deliver solutions tailored to client needs.
  • Ensure compliance with classification society standards and international maritime regulations.
  • Prepare technical reports, design documentation, and proposals for project approvals
  • Bachelor's /Masters degree in Naval Architecture
  • 3-5 years of experience in a marine design consultancy.
  • Proficiency in industry-standard software AutoCAD, MAXSURF, RHINO
  • Regulatory knowledge in IRS,DNV,RINA,ABS.
  • Strong problem-solving, analytical, and communication skills

Company offers expert design, engineering, and consulting services for a wide range of marine vessels. We specialize in creating custom solutions for ships, yachts, and boats, ensuring each vessel is safe, efficient, and built to meet industry standards
