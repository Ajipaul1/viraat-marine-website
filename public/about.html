VIRAAT
MARINE

Crafted with Passion

Welcome to Viraat Marine, an ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified company where competence and passion meets with the ocean. With a passion for marine design and fabrication, we specialize in crafting innovative solutions for a diverse range of marine applications. Our team of experienced engineers, managers and skilled craftsmen is dedicated to delivering high-quality, custom designs that meet the unique needs of our clients.

At Viraat Marine, we believe that every project is an opportunity to push the boundaries of creativity and technology. From conceptualization to fabrication and commissioning, we work closely with our clients to ensure that every detail is tailored to their specifications, ensuring optimal performance and durability.

Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest technology, allowing us to handle projects of all sizes—from small vessels to large-scale marine structures. Sustainability is at the core of our mission, and we strive to incorporate eco-friendly materials and practices into our processes. Whether you’re looking for innovative vessel designs, custom components, or complete fabrication services, Viraat Marine is your trusted partner in navigating the waters of marine excellence.
