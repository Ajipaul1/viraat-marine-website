About us

Welcome to Viraat Marine, an ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified company where competence and passion meets with the ocean. With a passion for marine design and fabrication, we specialize in crafting innovative solutions for a diverse range of marine applications. Our team of experienced engineers, managers and skilled craftsmen is dedicated to delivering high-quality, custom designs that meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Services

Our marine design services focus on creating efficient and innovative solutions for various marine vessels and structures. We leverage advanced engineering principles and modernistic technology to optimize performance and safety

Our marine fabrication involves the construction and assembly of marine structures and vessels using specialized materials and techniques. Our services include custom fabrication for boats, offshore platforms, and marine equipment, ensuring durability and performance in challenging environments.

Our skilled craftsmen utilize advanced techniques and high-quality materials to ensure durability and performance. We focus on custom solutions tailored to client specifications, ensuring each vessel meets specific needs and preferences. Emphasizing safety and craftsmanship, we adhere to industry standards throughout the building

Our efficient project managers escalate the planning, execution, and oversight of projects related to marine construction and commissioning. It ensures that projects are completed on time, within budget, and to specified quality standards. Key aspects include risk assessment, resource allocation, and adherence to safety regulations. Effective communication with stakeholders is essential for addressing challenges and ensuring project success. Our successful marine project management contributes to the safety and efficiency of marine operations and infrastructure.
