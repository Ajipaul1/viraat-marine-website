Viraat Tug-3200

Azimuth Stern Drive (ASD) Ocean Tugboat

Vessel Overview

The Viraat Tug-3200 is a heavy-duty 70-Ton bollard pull ASD tugboat designed for ship assist, offshore terminal towing, escort operations, and fire-fighting response.

General Parameters & Specifications

Length Overall (LOA)32.00 m
Beam Overall11.50 m
Draft4.80 m
Speed Performance13.5 Knots
Capacity / Payload70 Tonnes Bollard Pull
Main Propulsion2 x Niigata 6L28AHX (2000 kW each)
