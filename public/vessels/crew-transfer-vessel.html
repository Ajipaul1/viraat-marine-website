Viraat CTV-24

Offshore Crew Transfer Catamaran

Vessel Overview

The Viraat CTV-24 is a 24.5-meter high-speed catamaran specifically engineered for safe and efficient technician transfer to offshore wind farms, oil platforms, and marine installations.
